## Moxa ioLogik E2200 Series DIO Control

Control the Moxa ioLogik e2210 and e2212 

### Actions
-**Set DI Count Start**
-**Set DI Filter**
-**Set DI Mode**
-**Set DI Trigger**
-**Set DO Mode**
-**Set DO Pulse**
-**Set DO Status**

All actions can be performed against multiple DIs/DOs at once.

### Variables

-**DI # Counter**

-**Date**
-**Description**
-**Firmware Version**
-**IP Address**
-**Location**
-**MAC Address**
-**Model**
-**Serial Number**
-**Time**
